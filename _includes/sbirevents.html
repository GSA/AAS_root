SBIR Outreach Event - May 2023

Thursday, May 4th, 2023 10:00 AM – 11:00 AM ET (Midday)

REGISTER HERE

Join the GSA FAS AAS SBIR/STTR team as we provide an overview of GSA’s assisted acquisition services and how we can assist with meeting commercialization goals.

In this session, we’ll discuss:

  • GSA’s SBIR/STTR Intake Process
  • SBIR/STTR refresher with an emphasis on Phase III
  • GSA SBIR/STTR Update (all Phases)
  • GSA SBIR/STTR Initiatives (to include a potential Governmentwide IDIQ vehicle - RIO)



This event is best suited for anyone interested in learning more about GSA’s SBIR/STTR assisted acquisition services (e.g., potential clients, existing clients, SBIR contractors, Federal employees who want to learn more about GSA’s SBIR/STTR Phase III initiatives.)
