Please note that there are a few things that GSA FAS AAS offices are prohibited from buying, including weapons and weapons systems. GSAM 507.7001(a) allows GSA contracting activities to procure information technology supplies, services, and systems in support of a weapon system that is part of a national security system (e.g., components, services to install and maintain weapons systems, ancillary items and services). If your requirement is in support of a weapon system, it is important to ask some further questions to determine if it is, in fact, permissible for GSA to award.

Additionally, we are not authorized to award contracts subject to the Brooks Act; however, construction that is incidental to the in-scope SBIR work is permissible.

All other purchases can be made using other GSA vehicles.
