Event attendees will be responsible for managing calendar invites for this standing event. For the latest updates, please register for the AAS Second Tuesday GSA Interact Forum by selecting the "Request to Join" button.

AAS Navy, Air Force, Space Force Pipeline Reviews will occur on the Third Thursday of every month effective 06/20/2024 from 11:30 AM to 12:30 PM, Eastern Time.
Event attendees will be responsible for managing calendar invites for this standing event. For the latest updates, please register for the AAS Third Thursday GSA Interact Forum by selecting the "Request to Join" button.

AAS Civilian Pipeline Reviews will occur on the Fourth Wednesday of every quarter effective 07/24/2024 from 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM, Eastern Time.
Event attendees will be responsible for managing calendar invites for this standing event. For the latest updates, please register for the AAS Fourth Wednesday GSA Interact Forum by selecting the "Request to Join" button.
