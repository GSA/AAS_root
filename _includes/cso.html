CSO Program

The General Services Administration (GSA) established a program to competitively procure innovative commercial products and services using the Commercial Solutions Opening (CSO) procedure with a ceiling of $25 million per award.

Interested in learning more?

Government Clients

Our AAS team assists clients with many types of acquisitions and will work with you to determine the best acquisition strategy to fulfill your requirements. Reach out to us at AASInnovation@gsa.gov to see how we can help.

Industry Partners

You can respond to compete on an opportunity by submitting a brief about your solution. If there is a good match between your solution and our client's requirements, you will be asked to provide a full proposal.
