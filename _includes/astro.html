What is ASTRO?

ASTRO is a broadly scoped series of contract vehicles encompassing virtually all services supporting or involving manned, unmanned, or optionally manned platforms and robotics. ASTRO is administered by the General Services Administration’s (GSA) Office of Assisted Acquisition Service (AAS) FEDSIM Organization.

ASTRO is a family of ten separate, individual, Multiple Award (MA), Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contracts sponsored by the Department of Defense (DoD).

ASTRO At A Glance

  • Contracting Activities: ASTRO is a GSA AAS contract vehicle solution focused on DoD requirements. Only approved AAS contracting activities are authorized to use ASTRO.
  • Ordering Period for Task Orders: ASTRO task orders runs potentially through 2036.
  • Contract Types: All contract types can be utilized at the task order level, including cost reimbursement and hybrid models.
  • Ceiling: None.
  • Labor Categories: Standardized labor categories while still allowing for creation of customized labor categories at the task order level when necessary.
  • Ancillary Support: Yes, including Other Direct COsts (ODCs) at the task order level, as needed.
  • Service Types: Commercial, Non-commercial, classified and non-classified, CONUS and OCONUS.
  • Terms & Conditions: Allowance of specific terms and conditions/clauses/provisions at the task order level.
  • Total Awardees: 129 unique companies split into 10 pools ranging from 25-46 awardees in each pool.
  • Small Business Awardees: 37 awards to small business were made.
  • Service Contract Act Support Allowed: Yes.
  • What pools to use: In general, pools that contain the preponderance of the work should first be considered when considering an ASTRO solution.

Interested in ASTRO?

Access to ASTRO is available through Assisted Acquisition specifically through GSA’s Office of Assisted Acquisition Services (AAS) FEDSIM organization and any GSA AAS Contracting Officer (CO) granted a Delegation of Procurement Authority (DPA).

For more information, contact the ASTRO team at ASTRO@gsa.gov.
