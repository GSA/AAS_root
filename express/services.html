Our Services
 

EXPRESS is a program offered by GSAâ€™s Office of Assisted Acquisition Services (AAS). EXPRESS provides federal agencies with contract and financial management services that streamline acquisition delivery. We leverage acquisition expertise, tools and templates, and the EXPRESS platform to increase efficiency, reduce cost, and support industry innovation.

 
 

Mission

To support the missions of federal agencies by streamlining acquisition processes.

Vision

To transform the federal acquisition process by reducing time to award using our rapid acquisition methodology.

 

Features of the Program

Expedited Process

Average Procurement Administrative Lead Time (PALT) is 3 months depending on the requirement. Our process ensures federal agencies get to contract awards faster and efficiently.

Agency Control

The client determines the requirements and retains control of the project by maintaining their role as project managers.

Templated Process

We offer streamlined processes and procedures to ensure accuracy and efficiency for every project. We aim to provide time and cost savings to our federal clients.

EXPRESS Platform

Our customized workflow management platform allows our contracting staff, customers, and industry partners to collaborate quickly and seamlessly.

Small Business Opportunities

AAS EXPRESS provides opportunities for small business to participate in assisted acquisitions by emphasizing 8(a), Alliant Small Business, VETS 2, Oasis Small Business, Multiple Award Schedules, and NASA SEWP.
