Small Business

Did you know that one quarter of businesses that work with the Federal Government are small businesses? EXPRESS puts small businesses first by connecting with industry partners in the small business community and seeking creative ways to strengthen and grow this relationship.

EXPRESS focuses on nontraditional Government contractors and small businesses that provide innovative IT and professional services solutions—and gets them to award quickly. We use contract vehicles such as Alliant Small Business (Opens in a new window), OASIS Small Business (Opens in a new window), VETS 2 (Opens in a new window), 8(a) STARS II (Opens in a new window), GSA Schedule 70 (Opens in a new window), GSA Professional Services Schedule (Opens in a new window), and NASA SEWP (Opens in a new window).

The EXPRESS team offers in-person briefings. Contact us to learn how you can participate in EXPRESS opportunities and grow your business.
