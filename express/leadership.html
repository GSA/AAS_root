Our Leadership

Tom Howder

Assistant Commissioner, AAS

Tom Howder is the Assistant Commissioner of GSA's Office of Assisted Acquisition Services (AAS). Tom oversees the creation and implementation of the Express program and facilitates regional partnerships with AAS’ twelve Client Support Centers. Prior to his appointment as Assistant Commissioner, Tom served as the Deputy Assistant Commissioner for AAS. He has been with GSA for nearly 30 years.

Rob Coen

Director, Express

Rob Coen serves as the Director of Express and the Director of Strategy for FEDSIM. Rob manages strategic partnerships with federal agency customers and industry partners, develops innovative acquisition programs, and directs program expansion efforts in both the civilian and defense sectors. Prior to joining GSA, Rob was the Director of the NIH Information Technology Acquisition and Assessment Center (NITAAC).
