SBIR Innovative Acquisitions
 

The Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) program is a competitive program that encourages domestic businesses to engage in Federal research/research and development (R/R&D) that has the potential for commercialization. Participating Federal agencies partner with GSA EXPRESS to finalize and administer awards under SBIR Phase III to encourage businesses to commercialize their innovations. GSA EXPRESS provides SBIR agencies with expedited, templated processes that maintain acquisition quality and compliance from pre-award through post-award administration.

 

Our Process

 

You must be a SBIR Phase III eligible company and have access to the appropriate Phase I and II documentation.

 
 

We will work with a customer agency with a SBIR Phase III requirement to establish either a non-SBIR funded R&D or production contract for an eligible technology, product, service, and/or process.

 
 

We will assist with post-award administration.

 
 
 
 

FAQs

What is SBIR?

SBIR is a competitive program that encourages domestic businesses to participate in Federal research and development.

What is SBIR Phase III?

Phase III allows the business community to pursue commercialization objectives resulting from Phase I/II R/R&D activities. All Phase III R&D or production contracts for products, processes, or services intended for use by the U.S. Government or the private sector must be supported with non-SBIR funds.

what is the role of GSA EXPRESS?

In partnership with the Small Business Administration, GSA AAS is piloting an effort to assist in contract award and contract management of SBIR Phase III contracts on behalf of the DoD.
