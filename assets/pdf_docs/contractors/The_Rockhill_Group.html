Company Profile

The Rockhill Group, Inc. (TRG), is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), founded in 2004 by President and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) James Rock Rockhill. TRG has the capability to support complex contractual requirements and maintains a DCAA-approved accounting system. TRG has provided 24/7 aviation support operations for over 18 years to multiple geographically dispersed areas; as many as 37 locations at any given time, to include OCONUS. Our key customers include the United States Air Force (USAF) and the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA).
