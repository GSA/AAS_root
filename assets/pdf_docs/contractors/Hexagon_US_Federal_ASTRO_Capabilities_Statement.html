CAPABILITIES
Hexagon US Federal empowers US Federal Government Agencies— specifically Defense and Intelligence Organizations—to generate rapid and effective operational decisions by harnessing and analyzing vast amounts of complex data. For over five decades, we have developed and deployed innovative solutions that enable our customers to execute their missions effectively, safely, and with improved resilience. Hexagon US Federal brings an extensive depth of experience, skills, and facility and staff security clearances to our Federal Partners. With unique capabilities and skilled staff, we transform state-of-the-art commercial technology into mission-specific solutions for our customers—partnering with them to overcome their most challenging obstacles.
