Cruz Associates, Inc. (CAI) is a minority-owned, small business headquartered in Yorktown, VA with over 29 years of proven experience in providing technical support services to the United States Army, the United States Navy, the United States Special Operations Command (USSOCOM) and the Combat Capabilities Development Command (CCDC).  CAI and its over 285 employees worldwide, have the proven skills and experience to provide value added services to support Government agencies in meeting their operational objectives.  Services include the full spectrum of leadership, technical and management skills necessary to support strategic planning initiatives, technology integration, engineering, logistics and operational safety.  Specific functional capabilities include the following:

·       Requirements definition and concept development

·       Systems engineering

·       Acquisition and life cycle management

·       Program/project management

·       System development and qualification

·       Test coordination and execution

·       Airworthiness qualification plan development

·       Test Pilot services

·       Results presentation

·       Safety analyses

·       Aviation maintenance management

·       Logistics support

·       Field service engineering

·       Ordnance management and engineering

·       Production planning and support

·       Life cycle costing

·       Resource management

·       Explosives processing

 

Since 1998, CAI has been the primary support contractor to the U.S. Army Special Operations Aviation Command’s (USASOAC) as they modernize, transform, maintain, and sustain Special Operations Aviation (SOA) aircraft.  CAI provides extensive program management, maintenance management support, engineering support, and acquisition logistics support to SOA’s fleet of MH-60 Blackhawks, MH-47 Chinooks, MH-6 Little Birds, C-27 Spartan, and MQ-1C Gray Eagle Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS).  These services cover the entire spectrum of the Acquisition, Technology, and Logistics Life Cycle Management Framework from the Pre-Concept Phase to the Operations and Support Phase. 

CAI supports the CDCC with electrical and mechanical engineering and design support in the prototyping and installation of aircraft systems.  We provide flight test engineers to prove out those designs on wing.

CAI has provided comprehensive ordnance and energetics production, inventory management, logistics services, facilities maintenance, and engineering and administrative support to the Naval Surface Warfare Center – Yorktown, VA and Indian Head, MD (NSWC IHEODTD) since 1994.   

With a 40% veteran workforce representing over 2100 years of military service, CAI is undeniably committed to hiring veterans.  CAI is a four-time Hire Vets Platinum Medallion winner from the U.S. Department of Labor.

CAI was granted and has maintained a Top-Secret Facility Clearance since April 8, 2005.  We have passed all audits and inspections.  CAI has never had a security incident.  We currently perform work at the Top-Secret Level for two organizations.
